Why Seniors Are Vulnerable

Understanding the threats seniors face every day

Active Attacks

Phishing emails, tech support fraud, grandchild scams, bank impersonation — criminals specifically target senior trust.

Invisible Threats

Data breaches go unnoticed. Seniors reuse passwords and never know when accounts are compromised.

Financial Loss

Seniors lose more to fraud than any demographic. Losses are rising year-over-year with no end in sight.

Silent Suffering

Many victims don't report due to shame. DigiMaat removes stigma with support and guidance at every step.
